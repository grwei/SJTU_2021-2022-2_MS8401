Wavelet Applications

 

  • Image processing [Mallat (1988)]

  • Irregularly-spaced data (stellar activity) [Frick et al. (1997)]

  • 2D turbulence prediction; wavelet packet compression [Farge et al. (1992)]

  • Coherent structure detection [Farge (1992), Gamage (1990)]

  • Wave breaking & front detection [Liu (1994), Gamage & Blumen (1993)]

  • Wavelet bases for partial differential equations [Farge et al. (1992), Bacry et al. (1993)]

  • Paleoclimate [Bolton et al. (1995), Yiou et al. (1995)]
 

Summary

 

¤
Wavelet analysis is a useful addition to methods of geophysical data analysis.
¤
Most helpful in situations of locally-coherent signals, or non-stationary time series.
¤
More research is needed on the perils & pitfalls, choice of wavelet bases, and on statistical significance.
